=====================================================================
RATING & PENALTY LOGIC — AS IMPLEMENTED (updated 2026-07-10)
=====================================================================

DIMENSION SCORES (each learning item, 0-5, from the dropdown answers):
  Understanding : "understand clearly" 5 | "need significant support" 3 | "not understand" 2
  Examples      : "adequate…" 5 | "more…needed" 3
  Engagement    : "quite engaging" 5 | "needs more fun/engagement" 3
  Length        : "just the right length" 5 | "too long"/"too short" 3   (a SCORE, not a multiplier)
  Language      : "clear and age-appropriate" 5 | "text heavy"/"difficult words" 3
Practice/Exit options : "just right mix"/"fun and engaging" 5 | "too easy"/"too difficult"/"not engaging" 3
Overall (teacher)     : the teacher's own 1-5 rating ("4 - Very Good" -> 4)

ITEM RATING (learning):
  = average of the 5 dimensions across the teachers who reviewed that item,
    MINUS a SEVERITY-SCALED penalty applied AT THE ITEM LEVEL so it reflects and
    cumulates up into the section average. The error can come from ANY free-
    response box on this item (understanding / examples / engagement notes) OR
    from a reported error on this item; severity drives the size:
      -1.5  SEVERE   : incorrect answer/solution/image, wrong answer/solution,
                       "an error in", inaccurate, not correct, typo, mistake
      -1.0  MODERATE : misleading, not working / broken / bug, inconsistent,
                       does not match, not visible, cut off, overlap
      -0.5  MINOR    : confusing, unclear, hard to read/understand
      (most severe present wins — not stacked; constructive suggestions = no penalty)
  A wrong-lesson item ref (merged-cell typo) is dropped, not rated.

SECTION RATINGS (penalties are severity-scaled -0.5/-1.0/-1.5, same tiers as above):
  Learning = average of the item ratings (item penalties already cumulated in),
             minus a penalty if the lesson-wide "additional suggestions" flag an error.
  Practice = average of the practice option scores, minus a penalty if the
             practice "key observations" report an error.
  Exit     = same as Practice, from the exit "key observations".
  Overall  = average of the teachers' 1-5 rating.
  Classroom= average of the classroom-review answers (25% weight when available).

LESSON (final) RATING:
  = weighted blend of the sections that have data:
      Learning 40 | Practice 20 | Exit 5 | Overall 10 | Classroom 25
    Missing sections (e.g. no classroom) are dropped and the rest are rescaled.
  There is NO separate lesson-wide penalty — penalties live at the item/section
  level and cumulate into the sections, so the final always sits within the
  section range (never bizarrely below every section). Errors flagged in the
  lesson-wide "additional suggestions" are shown in the Errors Reported section,
  not applied as a hidden score penalty.

BANDS: Good >= 4.0 | Average 2.5-3.9 | Bad < 2.5
A lesson is PENDING until 3 teachers have reviewed it.
ERRORS REPORTED are shown as their own tab in each lesson and as a
program-level section on the home page.
